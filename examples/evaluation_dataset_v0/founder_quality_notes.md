# Evaluation Dataset v0 - Founder Quality Notes

Use these notes to judge whether later AI-assisted meaning layers are improving over the heuristic baseline.

- Strong outputs preserve source evidence and mention signal IDs.
- Strong clusters separate operational exception handling from compliance evidence chasing.
- Weak outputs overfit to generic "dashboard" or "AI assistant" ideas.
- Good opportunities identify a buyer or operator, not just a broad department.
- Good ideas include a repeatable workflow, a first wedge, and a plausible monetization route.
- Ambiguous and noisy signals should reduce confidence instead of being forced into confident opportunity claims.
