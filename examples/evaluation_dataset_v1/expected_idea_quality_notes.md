# Expected Idea Quality Notes v1

Target set: 15-25 idea variants across the 5 opportunity cards.

Quality expectations:

- Prefer specific wedge ideas over generic dashboards.
- Preserve linked signal IDs and opportunity IDs.
- Include at least two product shapes per strong opportunity when possible.
- Penalize generic assistant, generic dashboard, pure consulting, and founder-time-heavy services.
- Candidate ideas should identify a first experiment that can be run without a full product build.
