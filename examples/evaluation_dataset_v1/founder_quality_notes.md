# Founder Quality Notes v1

Founder ratings should be captured by AI stage using `good`, `okay`, `weak`, or `wrong`.

Suggested stage review questions:

- Signal understanding: did the extracted actor, pain, workaround, and cost signal match the source?
- Clustering: did recurrence reflect meaning rather than repeated words?
- Opportunity framing: did evidence and assumptions stay separated?
- Ideation: did ideas preserve traceability and avoid generic SaaS shapes?
- Critique: did the council find real ways the idea can die?

These notes are advisory and observational. They do not replace founder decisions.
