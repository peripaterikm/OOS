# 4.3 False-Positive Opportunity Suppressor

## Goal

Suppress opportunity candidates that sound plausible but are poorly grounded, generic, vendor/SEO-derived, buyerless, or mostly unsupported.

## Scope

- Added `src/oos/opportunity_false_positive_suppressor.py`.
- Added `OpportunityFalsePositiveAssessment` and `OpportunityFalsePositiveReason`.
- Added deterministic rules for generic AI dashboards, vendor/SEO copy, product submissions, disguised consulting/service-page copy, missing traceability, missing buyer/workaround, heavy unsupported assumptions, weak evidence sufficiency, and duplicate/non-recurring evidence risk.
- Integrated the suppressor narrowly into `src/oos/opportunity_quality_gate.py`.
- Added fixture-only tests in `tests/test_opportunity_false_positive_suppressor.py`.

## Behavior

- Critical false-positive assessments reject.
- High or medium false-positive assessments prevent `pass` and park for founder-safe review.
- Strong unpaid-invoice/cash-collection opportunities are not suppressed.
- YNAB/month-end reporting is not rejected as a false positive; it can remain kept or parked depending evidence gaps.
- Assessments preserve evidence IDs, source signal IDs, source URLs, matched patterns, reasons, and recommended next action.

## Out Of Scope

- No kill archive feature changes.
- No founder decision feedback loop.
- No live collection.
- No internet/API calls.
- No live LLM/API calls.
- No `provider.complete()` call.
- No auto-promotion or founder-decision replacement.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [x] Generic opportunities are downgraded.
- [x] Disguised consulting patterns are downgraded.
- [x] No-buyer ideas are parked/rejected.
- [x] Marketing-copy-derived opportunities are suppressed.
- [x] High/critical false-positive assessments prevent gate pass without auto-promotion.
- [x] Full validation passes.
