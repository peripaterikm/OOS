# 4.1 Opportunity Framing

## Goal

Turn semantic clusters into opportunity cards with evidence, assumptions, traceability, and a strict non-obvious angle.

## Context

Opportunity cards should not be pretty summaries without evidence. Unsupported claims must be assumptions.

## Inputs

- Semantic clusters
- Canonical signals
- Optional signal understanding records
- Optional contradiction reports
- Stubbed provider payloads

## Outputs

- `src/oos/opportunity_framing.py`
- `OpportunityCard`, `OpportunityEvidence`, `OpportunityAssumption`, and `OpportunityFramingResult`
- `evidence_missing` fallback behavior
- Focused and acceptance tests

## Key Decisions

- Evidence must link to valid signal IDs and cluster ID.
- Missing evidence parks the opportunity with `evidence_missing = true`.
- Keep opportunity framing standalone for now.

## Alternatives Rejected

- Treating unsupported provider claims as evidence.
- Implementing the quality gate in the framing mini-epic.

## Validation

- Opportunity framing tests.
- Acceptance guards for no live calls, no orchestrator wiring, and no source mutation.
- Full unittest discovery and `scripts/verify.ps1`.

## Known Limitations

- Cards are stub-provider outputs; quality depends on later provider and gate work.

## Next Step

Run opportunity cards through a deterministic quality gate.
