## Cleaner Behavior

- Normalizes whitespace in title and body.
- Normalizes URLs deterministically by lowercasing scheme/host, removing fragments, sorting query parameters, and trimming trailing slashes.
- Produces deterministic normalized content hashes.
- Preserves original title, body, source URL, and original content hash.
- Does not perform boilerplate removal.

## Classifier Behavior

- Rule-based only.
- Supports `pain_signal_candidate`, `workaround_signal_candidate`, `buying_intent_candidate`, `competitor_weakness_candidate`, `trend_trigger_candidate`, `needs_human_review`, and `noise`.
- Records matched rules, confidence, reason, human-review flag, and noise flag.
- Empty, near-empty, and obvious spam/noise can classify as `noise`.
- No evidence is deleted by classification.

## HN/GitHub Ambiguity Policy

Ambiguous non-empty `hacker_news_algolia` and `github_issues` evidence defaults to `needs_human_review`, not `noise`.
