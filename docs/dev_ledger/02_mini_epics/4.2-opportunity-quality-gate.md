# 4.2 Opportunity Quality Gate

## Goal

Prevent weak or pretty-but-empty opportunities from moving forward.

## Context

Pattern-guided ideation should receive grounded opportunities, not unsupported cards.

## Inputs

- `OpportunityCard` records from opportunity framing
- Gate criteria from Roadmap v2.2

## Outputs

- `src/oos/opportunity_quality_gate.py`
- `OpportunityGateDecision`, `OpportunityGateCriterionResult`, and `OpportunityGateResult`
- Deterministic pass/park/reject logic
- Focused and acceptance tests

## Key Decisions

- Gate is deterministic and advisory, not LLM-generated.
- Founder decision remains final authority.
- Missing target user, pain, or traceability rejects; missing evidence parks; missing wedge/uncertainty parks with warnings.

## Alternatives Rejected

- Wiring the gate into `run-signal-batch` during this support stage.
- Replacing founder review or founder decision flow.

## Validation

- Opportunity quality gate focused tests.
- Acceptance guards for no live calls, no orchestrator wiring, and founder authority.
- Full unittest discovery and `scripts/verify.ps1`.

## Known Limitations

- Gate thresholds are deterministic starting rules and may be refined after more real batches.

## Next Step

Use passed/parked/rejected opportunities to prepare pattern-guided ideation in item `5.1`.
